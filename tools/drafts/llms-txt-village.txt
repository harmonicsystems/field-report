# Village of Kinderhook, NY

> Official website of the Village of Kinderhook, an incorporated village in Columbia County, New York. Founded in the seventeenth century by Dutch settlers; the historic core is a designated National Register district.

## What this site is

The official municipal website of the Village of Kinderhook. Authoritative for: ordinances and local laws, meeting agendas and minutes, the village's business directory, parks and facilities, the village events calendar, and contact information for village offices and elected officials.

This site is run by the village government. Tourism promotion for the wider county is handled separately by [Columbia County Tourism](https://columbiacountytourism.org/).
