# Chapter 130, Article VIII — Zoning Board of Appeals (key facts cited)

The Village ZBA is **not** a separate chapter in the Village code — it lives
inside the Zoning chapter, which is the standard NY Village Law pattern.

## § 130-38 Continuation; membership; staff; procedures

[Amended 5-26-1977 by L.L. No. 3-1977; 8-11-1993 by L.L. No. 1-1993;
2-14-1996 by L.L. No. 1-1996; 9-10-2003 by L.L. No. 2-2003;
3-9-2022 by L.L. No. 1-2022.]

> The Village Zoning Board of Appeals shall be continued and, from the
> date of enactment of this chapter, shall consist of five members. The
> Mayor shall appoint the Board of Appeals and the Chairperson thereof,
> subject to the approval of the Board of Trustees. The Village Board
> shall have the power to remove any member of the Board of Appeals for
> cause and after public hearing.

- Five regular members; Mayor appoints with Board of Trustees approval.
- Mayor appoints the Chairperson.
- Five-year terms, staggered (one expires each Village year).
- Optional alternate member, by Village Board appointment.
