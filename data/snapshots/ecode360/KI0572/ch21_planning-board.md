# Chapter 21 — Planning Board (key facts cited)

## § 21-1 Establishment of Board

[Adopted 3-3-1961 (§ 20-1 of the 1978 Code); amended 3-9-2022 by L.L. No. 1-2022.]

> Pursuant to § 7-718 of the Village Law of the State of New York, a
> Planning Board of five members shall be appointed for the Village of
> Kinderhook, Columbia County, New York.

- Five regular members, optional one alternate (two-year term).
- Alternate substitutes for an absent regular member or one with a conflict
  of interest, by Chairperson assignment recorded in minutes.
- Quorum: standard majority of full membership; majority vote of the entire
  Board required to adopt any action.
- Subsequent sections (§ 21-2, § 21-3) cover terms and other procedure;
  cite live URL when needed.
