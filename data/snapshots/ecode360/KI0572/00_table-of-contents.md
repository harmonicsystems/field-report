## Editor's notes for this project

- The Village of Kinderhook is a separate municipality from the Town of
  Kinderhook (KI0583). Properties inside the Village are governed by both
  codes; in case of conflict the Village code controls Village land.
- The Village TOC has **no separate ZBA chapter** in the Administrative Part.
  The Zoning Board of Appeals is established and governed inside Chapter 130
  (Zoning), which is the standard New York Village Law pattern.
- There is **no separate Historic Preservation Commission chapter** — the
  Commission is established inside Chapter 75 (Landmarks and Historic Districts).
